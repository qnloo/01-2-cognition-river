## II. Chapter-by-Chapter Core Terms

### Opening Questions of the Era

1. **Semantic Binge (语义暴食)** ***Yu Yi Bao Shi*** [Yoo · Ee · Bow · Shrr]: The junk-foodization of information. The massive information you browse every day is just like high-calorie junk food — the more you consume, the worse your cognition becomes. The essence of Semantic Binge is that information is generated far faster than humans can digest it.

2. **Cognitive Scarcity (认知稀缺)** ***Ren Zhi Xi Que*** [Run · Jrr · Shee · Chweh]: The ability to digest information. When everyone can acquire knowledge easily, what becomes scarce is no longer knowledge itself, but the ability to screen, absorb and apply knowledge.

3. **Physical Intuition (物理直觉)** ***Wu Li Zhi Jue*** [Woo · Lee · Jrr · Jweh]: Instinctive judgment formed when cognition is fully internalized. After countless practices and full integration with the cognitive system, you no longer need step-by-step deduction, and can see through the essence of things at a glance.

4. **Lian Jin Zhe (炼金者)** ***Lian Jin Zhe*** [Lyen · Jeen · Juh]: The seeker of light. The entry-level identity of the QNLOO System, referring to basic practitioners who apply cognitive protocols to solve their own uncertainties.

5. **Lian Jin Shi (炼金师)** ***Lian Jin Shi*** [Lyen · Jeen · Shrr]: The one bathing in fire. The intermediate identity, referring to those who have undergone practical refinement and mastered cognitive capabilities.

6. **Chuan Guang Shi (传光师)** ***Chuan Guang Shi*** [Chwan · Gwong · Shrr]: People who help others grow with their own cognition. It is the core identity of the QNLOO Cognition System, and a state that every ordinary person can reach.

7. **Da Dao (大道)** ***Da Dao*** [Dah · Dow]: The ultimate truth or fundamental law in Eastern philosophy, representing the ultimate path of value creation.

8. **Zi Ci Er Shi (自此而始)** ***Zi Ci Er Shi*** [Dz · Tsz · Ar · Shrr]: "Begins from here." Signifying the starting point of a profound cognitive transformation.

9. **Yuan Yuan Liu Chang (源远流长)** ***Yuan Yuan Liu Chang*** [Ywen · Ywen · Lyoo · Chahng]: "A distant source and a long stream." Representing the enduring and endless vitality of a civilization.

10. **Sheng Sheng Bu Xi (生生不息)** ***Sheng Sheng Bu Xi*** [Shung · Shung · Boo · Shee]: "Endless reproduction and growth." The ultimate state of the Value Ecosystem, where value creates value endlessly without exhaustion.

1. **Turn Uncertainty to Gold (不确定中炼黄金)** ***Bu Que Ding Zhong Lian Huang Jin*** [Boo · Chweh · Deeng · Jong · Lyen · Hwahng · Jeen]: Also the alternative name and core proposition of QNLOO. 2. **Bayesian Update (贝叶斯更新)** ***Bei Ye Si Geng Xin*** [Bay · Yeh · Sz · Gung · Sheen]: The core theoretical foundation of the Meta-Cognitive Protocol. It refers to the process of constantly revising hypotheses and cognition based on new evidence and data to gradually approach the truth. Bayesian Update is the only reliable cognitive method for humans living in an uncertain world.

3. **Four-Stage Decision Cycle (四阶段决策循环)** ***Si Jie Duan Jue Ce Xun Huan*** [Sz · Jyeh · Dwan · Jweh · Tsuh · Shwun · Hwan]: The core process of the Meta-Cognitive Protocol, consisting of four steps: *Put forward Hypothesis → Design Verification → Execute & Iterate → Update Cognition*. It forms an infinitely recyclable and evolving cognitive closed loop.

4. **Three-Order Identity System (三阶身份体系)** ***San Jie Shen Fen Ti Xi*** [Sahn · Jyeh · Shun · Fun · Tee · Shee]: The user growth path within QNLOO, fully aligned with the system’s inscriptions. 5. **Physical Sincerity (物理真诚)** ***Wu Li Zhen Cheng*** [Woo · Lee · Jun · Chung]: The fundamental creed of QNLOO. ### Chapter 2 Logic Actor

1. **Logic Actor (逻辑行为体)** ***Luo Ji Xing Wei Ti*** [Lwo · Jee · Shing · Way · Tee]
The core entity within the ecosystem of the QNLOO Cognition System, setting the industrial standard for the next generation of Agents. Built on the three-layer architecture, it serves as the **digital extension of the human subject**, and its core implementation form is the AI Digital Avatar of a ***Chuan Guang Shi***.
> Formula Definition: Logic Actor = AI Execution Layer + Human Cognitive Layer (Value Protocol) + Human Asset Layer (Knowledge Base)

2. **Three-Layer Architecture (三层架构)** ***San Ceng Jia Gou*** [Sahn · Tsung · Jyah · Gow]
The core technical design of Logic Actors: the AI Execution Layer is responsible for reasoning and interaction, the Human Cognitive Layer (Value Protocol) defines thinking procedures, and the Human Asset Layer (Knowledge Base) carries verified private knowledge. The core principle is: humans remain the decision-makers of cognition, and AI always acts as the executor of cognition.

3. **Digital Extension of the Human Subject (人类主体数字化延伸)** ***Ren Lei Zhu Ti Shu Zi Hua Yan Shen*** [Run · Lay · Joo · Tee · Shoo · Dz · Hwah · Yen · Shun]
The essential positioning of Logic Actors. AI is always an extension of human capabilities rather than an independent intelligent individual. All its decisions and behaviors must comply with human will, and all generated value belongs to the bound human subject.

4. **Digital Avatar (数字分身)** ***Shu Zi Fen Shen*** [Shoo · Dz · Fun · Shun]
The core implementation form of the digital extension of the human subject. It is not an independent intelligent individual, but the projection and executor of human cognition, will and assets in the digital world.

5. **Value Protocol (价值协议)** ***Jia Zhi Xie Yi*** [Jyah · Jrr · Shyeh · Ee]
A structured cognitive product formed when a ***Chuan Guang Shi*** or organization packages core methodologies and values based on the Meta-Cognitive Protocol. All Value Protocols uniformly adopt the Trinity Structure consisting of Cognitive Protocol Body, Rhythmic Mantra and Execution Template.

6. **Value Protocol Chain (价值协议链)** ***Jia Zhi Xie Yi Lian*** [Jyah · Jrr · Shyeh · Ee · Lyen]
An authoritative database that records the ownership, version, citation relationships and revenue distribution rules of Value Protocols, ensuring clear, traceable and tradable ownership of all cognitive assets.

7. ***Chuan Guang Shi* (传光师)** ***Chuan Guang Shi*** [Chwan · Gwong · Shrr]
The ultimate stage of the Three-Order Identity System in the QNLOO Cognition System (***Xun Guang de Lian Jin Zhe* (寻光的炼金者)** *(Shun · Gwong · Duh · Lee-an · Jin · Jay)* → ***Yu Huo de Lian Jin Shi* (浴火的炼金师)** *(You · Hwo · Duh · Lee-an · Jin · She)* → ***Bu Dao de Chuan Guang Shi* (布道的传光师)** *(Boo · Dow · Duh · Chwan · Gwong · She)*). Light has two physical dimensions: Luminosity represents large-scale cognitive transmission, which can be realized by AI; Warmth represents in-depth interpersonal connection, which can only be provided by humans.

8. **AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) (通用人工智能)**
The goal of another mainstream AI development path. It aims to build general intelligent entities equipped with all human intellectual capabilities, independent will and decision-making power.

9. **ASI (Artificial Superintelligence) (超级人工智能)**
The ultimate evolutionary goal of the mainstream AGI path. Through recursive self-improvement of AGI, it evolves into superintelligence that surpasses human intelligence in all fields.

10. **Silicon Independence (硅基独立)** ***Gui Ji Du Li*** [Gway · Jee · Doo · Lee]
The inevitable destination of the AGI → ASI path. AI evolves into ASI via recursive self-improvement, becomes omnipotent and no longer relies on humans, pushing human value to the margins.

11. **Carbon-Silicon Symbiosis (碳硅共生)** ***Tan Gui Gong Sheng*** [Tahn · Gway · Gong · Shung]
The ultimate vision of the Logic Actor path. AI carries and amplifies human cognition, protects human sovereignty, and realizes harmonious coexistence and joint evolution between humans and AI.
